Featrue List

  • Citation network - A network consisting of papers as nodes and co-citations as links
    1. To view citations of any selected paper, right click on the paper node select "Citations" option and enter the number of results to view.
    2. In case of unavailabilty of the citation information for the chosen paper an alert is shown.
  • Collaboration network - A network consisting of authors as nodes and co-authorship as links
    1. To view Collaboration network of any selected author, right click on the author node select "Co-authorship" option and enter the number of results to view.
    2. In case of unavailabilty of the co-authorship information for the chosen author an alert is shown.
  • Citing non-coauthors - A network consisting of authors who've cited selected authors work but not the co-authors of a selected author
    1. To view Citing non-coauthors network of any selected author, right click on the author node select "Citing non-coauthors" option and enter the number of results to view.
    2. In case of unavailabilty of the Citing non-coauthors information for the chosen author an alert is shown.
  • Authorship - View papers authored by a selected author
    1. To view authorship network of any selected author, right click on the author node select "Paper authored" option and enter the number of results to view.
    2. In case of unavailabilty of the authorship information for the chosen author an alert is shown.
